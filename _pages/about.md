I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, advised by [Prof. Lui Sha](https://cs.illinois.edu/about/people/faculty/lrs), at [Cyber-Physical Systems Integration Lab](https://cps-il.github.io/).

My research focusses on making Real-Time and Cyber-Physical Systems safe and secure. This includes functional safety, temporal safety and system security.
